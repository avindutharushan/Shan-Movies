Pravegaya (2015) Fight to Survive

Sinhala Action Crime
12 June 2015 145 minutes
7.1

Plot Summary

Hemal, a carefree guy, buys a TVS Apache bike with his father’s savings, sparking a chain of events that drags him into the underworld. His bike is stolen, leading him to cross paths with ruthless gang leader Brando and his treacherous brother, G.J.I. When Hemal witnesses a murder, he becomes a target, and his father is gravely injured in the ensuing conflict. Brando seeks peace but is betrayed and killed by G.J.I, who then sets his sights on Hemal. In a final showdown at an ice factory, Hemal defeats G.J.I, protecting his family. Forever changed, Hemal rides away on his beloved bike.
