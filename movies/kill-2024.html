Plot Summary

Amrit Rathod is a NSG commando, who upon learning that his girlfriend Tulika is engaged against her will, boards a New Delhi-bound train with her in a quest to stop the forced marriage. When a gang of knife-wielding bandits led by Fani begins to terrorize the passengers on the train, Amrit and his friend Viresh take on the bandits to save the passengers, and a brutal fight ensues.
